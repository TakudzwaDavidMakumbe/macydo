Overview

About

My Africa Child and Youth Development Organization is a Humanitarian Organization aiming to empower young people and groom them to become influential leaders and change-makers in their countries, working towards an economically independent African continent.

Our Mission

At My Africa Child and Youth Development Organization (MACYDO), our mission is to empower young people by providing them with the knowledge, skills, and opportunities needed to become influential leaders and change-makers in their communities.

Our History

Our History

My Africa Child and Youth Development Organization (MACYDO) was founded with a vision to empower young people and shape them into influential leaders who drive positive change in their communities and beyond. Since its inception, MACYDO has been dedicated to fostering an economically independent African continent by equipping youth with the necessary skills, knowledge, and opportunities to thrive.

Our Certificates

Between 2012 and 2014, MACYDO received multiple recognitions and certifications for its outstanding contributions to youth empowerment and development. These certifications affirmed our commitment to fostering education, leadership, entrepreneurship, and innovation among young Africans.

2012-2018. Our Establishment

Between 2012 and 2014, MACYDO received multiple recognitions and certifications for its outstanding contributions to youth empowerment and development. These certifications affirmed our commitment to fostering education, leadership, entrepreneurship, and innovation among young Africans.

2019. The Early Days

In 2014, MACYDO was officially established as a fully structured humanitarian organization with a clear mission: to provide young people with opportunities to realize their full potential through impactful projects in various sectors.

2020-2024. Prosperity of Company

The journey began with small-scale initiatives focused on leadership training, mentorship programs, and educational workshops. Through dedication and resilience, MACYDO quickly gained momentum, attracting support from local communities, educators, and young visionaries eager to make a difference..
