Schedule of Event

  • 09:00-10:00
    Check-in

    Guest check-in, badge collection, and event material distribution. Networking over tea/coffee Exhibitor booths open.

  • 10:00-11:00
    Opening Ceremony

    Welcome Address by MACYDO LEAD Representative. Keynote Speech: “The Future is Now – Empowering Africa’s Youth Entrepreneurs” Guest of Honor Speech (Vusi Thembekwayo)

  • 11:00-13:00
    High-Level Panel Discussion

    Topic: “Africa’s Next Generation Entrepreneurs: Opportunities & Challenges”. Panelists: Industry leaders, successful young entrepreneurs, policymakers. Moderated discussion on entrepreneurship trends, policies, and growth strategies

  • 13:00-14:00
    Lunch & Networking

    Attendees enjoy lunch while connecting with investors, exhibitors, and fellow entrepreneurs

  • 14:00-17:00
    Awards & Recognition Ceremony

    Best Innovative Startup, Most Promising Entrepreneur, Social Impact Business Award, Young Business Leader of the Year
